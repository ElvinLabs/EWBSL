5th International Conference on Structural Engineering and Construction Management


5th International Conference on Sustainable built Environment will be successfully held from the 12th to 15th of December 2014 at Earlâ€™s Regency Hotel, Kandy celebrating the innovative research and novel experiences associated to a number of topics related to Sustainability under 12 parallel sessions.

The Vision of this Conference is to drive innovative research for tomorrow's Development. The conference will feature presentations by authors of all accepted papers, as well as keynote lecturers. General and plenary sessions will be accompanied by workshops and technical sessions. Accepted papers will be published in a special volume of proceedings with an international standard book number (ISBN). main themes of the Conference will be ,

  • Sustainable Buildings & Infrastructures
  • Green Energy
  • Climate Change
  • Green Technology
  • Community, Environment and the Ecosystem

The conference will be Co Chaired by Professor Ranjith Dissanayake, Professor Priyan Mendis, Professor Thishan Jayasinghe, Professor Janaka Ruwanpura and Eng. Shiromal Fernando. This will be brought forth as a joint venture of University of Peradeniya, University of Moratuwa and University of Ruhuna. We hope that it will be more enthusiastic and a step ahead in Sustainable Engineering.
